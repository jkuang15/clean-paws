What is Clean Paws?

Clean Paws is a smart recycling bin that is designed to enhance the efficiency of recyclable sorting. It utilizes technology to address environmental challenges, offering an intelligent and convenient solution for effective recycling. This system integrates a metal detector, a camera, and machine learning algorithms to identify and categorize items into 3 distinct bins: metal, paper, and miscellaneous. Our key components include the metal detector, camera system, machine learning, and sorting mechanism.

How does it work?

As metallic items pass through the bin, the detector triggers the sorting mechanism to divert them to the designated metal bin after the camera microphone detects the metal detector's signal frequency. Using a webcam connected to our computer, the camera will generate a live feed for the machine learning model to recognize and classify paper objects based on visual characteristics. The sorting mechanism will be triggered based on the response received from the metal detectors and live feed camera. it will sort the items into their respective bins based on the classification provided by our program. If a signal is not received from the metal detector nor from the paper identifying model, the item will be sorted into the Miscellaneous bin.
